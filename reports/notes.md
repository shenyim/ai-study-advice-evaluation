# EDA Notes

## Dataset Overview
The cleaned dataset contains 14 unique student questions.

## Main Categories
The most common categories are career_planning and data_science.

## Risk Distribution
The dataset includes low, medium, and high-risk questions. High-risk questions mainly appear in phd_planning and career_planning.

## Key Insight
AI answers to technical questions may be easier to evaluate, while AI answers to PhD and career planning questions require stronger risk control because they may influence important student decisions.

## Next Step
The next step is to generate AI answers for each question and evaluate them using a human-centered rubric.
